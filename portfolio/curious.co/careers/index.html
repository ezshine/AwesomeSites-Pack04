Art Director

Overview

Were you the kid who licked a 9-volt battery just for the hell of it? Do you spend more time making your Halloween costume than you do wearing it? If so, welcome to the club. As a Curious Creative, you’ll be invited to pack your bags and head for the Hollywood Hills. We’ll learn from you. You’ll learn from us. It'll be wonderful.

We're looking for an Art Director with solid compositing skills, great concepting smarts, and an ability to convey complex messaging through images, graphics, and type. If you think you've got what it takes, we'd love to meet you. Be ready to show us a book full of work that is as smart as it is pretty.

Responsibilities

  • At least 3 years of design agency experience.
  • Strong conceptual, design and typography skills.
  • Strong creative problem-solving skills with the desire to learn and assist others on your team.
  • Ability to articulate creative ideas and “sell” your work to a group of your peers.
  • A positive attitude that contributes to the overall success of a collaborative environment.

Digital Strategist

Overview

We are looking for a team member with a solid foundation and proven track record managing social campaigns and implementing creative approaches to tell brand stories across social. To achieve success the Digital Strategist will have the ability to build social strategies, manage day-to-day execution, cultivate and grow relationships across clients, agents, talent/influencers and have their finger on the pulse of emerging trends impacting our industry.

The ideal candidate will build strong social strategies for consumer clients and lead/oversee various elements of execution including day-to-day client communication, contract negotiations, campaign brief development and measurement. They will be digitally-attuned with a POV on how we can incorporate emerging industry techniques and trends into our work.

Responsibilities

  • Lead end-to-end execution and planning of social marketing campaigns and understand the nuances of the campaign management process.
  • Lead and oversee day-to-day operations of active social campaigns, including brief development, influencer communications, contract negotiations, content development management and campaign reporting.
  • Support team lead across various larger campaign proposals
  • Identify, establish and manage ongoing opportunities for brands to engage with influencers and other relevant partners
